About Me

Hello! I'm Pawara Gamage

I'm Pawara Gamage, a 3rd-year Electro-mechanical Engineering student at the Faculty of Technology, University of Ruhuna. have a strong foundation in both electrical and mechanical systems, and enjoy integrating them to solve real-world problems. My practical experience includes working with ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and various electronic components.

I've completed several projects such as an automated greenhouse system, a vertical hydroponic tower, a micromouse maze solver robot, a robotic arm with object detection, and a home automation system. These projects improved my skills in circuit design, automation, and programming.

I'm passionate about learning new technologies and continuously working on innovative electronic-related projects, especially in automation and smart agriculture.
